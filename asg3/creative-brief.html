Creative Brief

Company: ScreemStream

Discription

I want to make a streaming service that is dedicated to horror films, offering a blood-curdling collection of classics, cult favorites, international horror and indie nightmares.

Release Date: Fall 2025
Problem: The only problem with creating a new streaming service is licensing, competition with Shudder, Limited Audience, Age verification, Technical Infrastructure, and Marketing.
Audience This streaming service idea is targeted for die-hard horror fans.
Competition To rival against streaming services like Shudder, Tubi, Peacock, and amc+.
Key Messages To promote this platform by spreading a slogan like Your Home for Horror, Horror Lives Here, or More Than Movies, It’s a Horror Experience.
Call to Action A call to action is getting the users action with a free trail that writes, “Start your free 31-day trail, IF YOU DARE!” This will encourage users to test the service with no risk while tying into the spooky theme.
How to Measure Success By developing this streaming service, we will be able to gain subscriber growth, user engagement, revenue ratings, and a growing community.
