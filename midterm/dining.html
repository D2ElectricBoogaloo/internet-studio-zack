The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild Dining Experience

A journey through Hyrule’s most iconic regions, reimagined as a gourmet feast.

STARTERS — The Traveler’s Beginning

Hylian Wild Mushroom Skewer A medley of roasted forest mushrooms glazed with herb-infused butter and served on a charred wooden skewer. Inspired by the first meal every adventurer cooks.

Endura Carrot Bisque Silky carrot soup spiced with saffron and topped with a drizzle of cream and edible gold leaf. Boost your stamina before your quest begins.

Korok Forest Salad Mixed forest greens, roasted nuts, and hearty fruits tossed in a light apple blossom dressing. Hidden Korok seed garnish not included.

MAINS — The Champion’s Feast

Seared Prime Meat & Seafood Fry Tender steak medallions paired with pan-seared prawns, served with roasted root vegetables and a honey glaze. A hearty meal fit for a hero on the road.

Goron Fire-Roasted Rock Steak Smoky, char-grilled ribeye marinated with volcanic salts from Death Mountain. Served sizzling hot — handle with care!

Rito Herb-Roasted Fowl Succulent roasted bird seasoned with mountain herbs and served with wild berry reduction. A soaring dish from the skies of Hebra.

Zora’s Domain Salmon Meunière Pan-seared salmon with lemon butter caper sauce and seaweed crisps. As elegant as the waters of the Lanayru region.

SIDES — Forest & Field

Steamed Swift Carrots For extra speed and agility.

Hearty Radish Mash Creamy mashed radish with roasted garlic and spring onions.

Tabantha Wheat Rolls Warm rolls baked with local grains and served with honey butter.

DESSERTS — Sweet Rewards

Snowquill Parfait Layered berries, vanilla custard, and frosted cream — a chill from the peaks of Hebra.

Noble Pursuit Sorbet Refreshing desert fruit sorbet inspired by Gerudo Town’s most famous (and exclusive) drink.

Silent Princess Cheesecake A delicate lavender-infused cheesecake with a white chocolate glaze. Rare, beautiful, and unforgettable.

DRINKS — Potions & Elixirs

Energizing Elixir Citrus and mint mocktail with sparkling water and a dash of stamina syrup.

Spicy Pepper Tea Warm cinnamon-pepper blend to keep the cold at bay.

Hearty Durian Wine Fermented tropical fruit with a bold, legendary flavor.

>Fairy Fountain Cocktail (21+) A shimmering violet drink with elderflower, gin, and a hint of magic dust.
